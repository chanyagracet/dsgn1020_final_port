Playing around with different generative art tools, I found one
that constant changes the cross-section of our "circle" according
to different cursor movements (ie. speed). When picked the color red
for it, it reminded me of spilled ketchup. Hence,
I added a random generator and counter as a way of randomizing the
shades of color being used each time and messages appear depending
on which color is picked.
